What is it?

It is a HTML5 boilerplate (Starter Template) including:

  • Bourbon v4.2.6
  • Jquery v2.2.2
  • Bootstrap v3.3.6
  • Outdated_Browser v1.1.3
  • Font_Awesome v4.5.0
  • Google Analytics
